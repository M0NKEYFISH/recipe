Chocolate chip cookies

My favourite recipe for cookies

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • 150g Brown Sugar
  • 50g White Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Honey
  • 2 teaspoons of Vanilla extract
  • 1 Egg
  • 200g Plain Flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 1/3 teaspoon of Salt
  • 200g Chocolate Chips (any chocolate you like)

Oven Temp

175c (350f)

Method

  1. Soften butter for a few seconds in microwave or at room temperature. Mix well with sugar until creamy and smooth.
  2. Mix in the Honey, Vanilla and egg lightly with a wooden spoon.
  3. Seive in the Flour, Salt and Baking Soda into the mixture and mix until the dough has formed and sticks together. (add more flour as needed)
  4. Add half of the Chocolate Chips into the dough.
  5. Cover the mix and put into the refrigerator for 10 minutes to cool.
  6. Form the dough into small balls and place onto a baking sheet spread out so there is plenty of room between them and add a few of the extra Chocolate Chips on top of each ball.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes. the cookis should still be a little pale in the middle and te sides just beginning to brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for at least 15 minutes
Variations

Try replacing the egg with a very ripe Banana or adding ginger or cinnamon to create new flavours.

Swap the chocolate chips for any other sweet treats you would like to try.

Share cookies with friends, family, co-workers and neighbors to spread some love and happiness*
